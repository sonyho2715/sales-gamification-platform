### **2. PROFIT TRACKING, NOT JUST REVENUE**
**Priority:** HIGH
**Why:** Revenue doesn't pay bills - profit does

**What I Need:**
- Gross margin % per salesperson
- Profit per transaction (not just revenue)
- GMROI (Gross Margin Return on Investment) tracking
- Show who's selling high-margin items vs. discounted clearance

**Why It Matters:**
- Johnny might have $50K in sales, but if it's all clearance at 10% margin, he's less valuable than Sarah with $40K at 40% margin
- I need to reward profitable behavior, not just volume

### **3. CUSTOMER-LEVEL ANALYTICS**
**Priority:** HIGH
**Why:** Repeat customers are 5x cheaper to sell to than new customers

**What I Need:**
- Track repeat customer rate per salesperson
- Customer satisfaction scores (post-purchase survey integration)
- Referral tracking (who generates word-of-mouth?)
- "Sticky" salespeople metrics (customers who ask for them by name)

**Why It Matters:**
- Short-term sales spikes are great, but I need long-term customer relationships
- Some salespeople are great closers but terrible at follow-up
- NPS (Net Promoter Score) predicts my business growth
